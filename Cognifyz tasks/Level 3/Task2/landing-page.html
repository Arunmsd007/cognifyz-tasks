About the Internship

Join Cognifyz Technologies for an exciting Web Developer Internship program! This internship offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in web development, working on real-world projects under the guidance of experienced mentors. The program runs for your selection of 1 or 2 months, with flexible start dates.

Benefits of the Internship

  • Real-World Experience And Mentorship
  • Work on cutting-edge technologies and tools.
  • Receive mentorship from industry experts.
  • Enhance your portfolio with live projects.
  • Opportunity for a full-time role based on performance.

Desired Skills And Technologies

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks like React or Angular.
  • Basic understanding of back-end technologies (Node.js, Python, etc.).
  • Knowledge of version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
